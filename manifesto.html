Sparkwood and 21 manifesto.

Above all, SPARKWOOD AND 21 wants to make digital and analogue games that are fun to play.

SPARKWOOD AND 21 wants to leave the world in a better place that they found it. We want to develop sustainable business practices from the outset. We want to ensure that we are doing everything to help work with the environment and culture around us, rather than against it.

SPARKWOOD AND 21 is committed to being as inclusive and accessible as possible.

SPARKWOOD AND 21 aims to develop its products where possible with privacy in mind. If we don’t need your data, we don’t ask for it.

SPARKWOOD AND 21 believes that diversity is the key to creativity, growth and community. We try to ensure that we are a safe space for all.
