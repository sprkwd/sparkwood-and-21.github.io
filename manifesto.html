sprkwd manifesto.

Above all, SPRKWD wants to help SPARKWOOD AND 21 make digital and analogue games that are fun to play. It does this by making products and tools that facilitate this.

SPRKWD will open source a majority of these products and tools so that others can benefit from using them.

SPRKWD wants to leave the world in a better place that they found it. We want to develop sustainable business practices from the outset. We want to ensure that we are doing everything to help work with the environment and culture around us, rather than against it.

SPRKWD is committed to being as inclusive and accessible as possible.

SPRKWD aims to develop its products where possible with privacy in mind. If we don’t need your data, we don’t ask for it.

SPRKWD believes that diversity is the key to creativity, growth and community. We try to ensure that we are a safe space for all.

Now, go and take a look at our tools. Alternatively, you can go and see some of the games we helped develop.
